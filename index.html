PANCAKES or CREPES or MLYNTSI

You will need

  • 4 eggs
  • 1 glass of milk
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 (or 2) tablespoon sugar
  • 10 ml vegetable oil
  • 50 ml sparkling water (optional)

What to do with all that

  1. Combine the ingredients Whisk to combine.
  2. With an electric mixer, beat until well combined.
  3. Heat a nonstick skillet over medium-high and spray with nonstick cooking spray.
  4. Ladle the batter into the pan just to cover the bottom. Cook until the surface begins to bubble, then flip over to cook the other side.
  5. Serve hot with desired toppings.
